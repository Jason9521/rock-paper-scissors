Rules of Rock Paper Scissors

The rules of the game are simple. You will choose from the following three options; rock, paper or scissors. The computer will then select an option at random.

Rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, and paper beats rock. Whoever reaches a score of 5 first wins the game. Good luck!
